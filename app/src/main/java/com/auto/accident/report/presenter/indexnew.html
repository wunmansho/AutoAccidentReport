Accident Report - Advanced AI Speech to text. All the convenience of your voice automated home and office. Use Accident Report with the same confidence that you have in your other voice automated products. Life isn't always a beach, but we can still enjoy the comforts of home in the worst of situations. Accident Report is an application that allows any driver or passenger to quickly gather critical detail information at an accident scene. Many times drivers exchange only minimal information and leave it to the police report to fully describe the accident. Often, the accident report will omit critical details or fail to capture convincing evidence that may help the driver to protect themself in the event of a lawsuit or assist the driver in being fairly compensated for losses resulting from the neglegence of others. Accident Report allows you to gather information quickly using your device speech to text capeability or by using the std device keyboard. Information about Individuals including drivers, passengers, witnesses and police officers may be gatherd as quickly as you can speak. The same is true for involved vehicles. Pictures may be taken and associated with the individual, vehicle or accident in general to help keep a clear record of the scene. Insurance information about policy coverage for vehicles and individuals may be gathered along with any third party information in the case of a commercial accident. A detailed accident report may be printed in PDF format. This may be used as a handy reference by attorneys, employers, or third party investigators to quickly get an accurate picture of what happened and who was involved. When a serious accident occurs it is a great comfort knowing that you have more than the word of the involved parties and the police report.
